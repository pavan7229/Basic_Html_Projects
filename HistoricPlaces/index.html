Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra,built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal

Agra Fort

Agra Fort is a historical fort in the city of Agra in India.It was the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal Dynasty till 1638

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in the city of Delhi in India.Every year on the Independence day of India,the Prime Minister hoists the Indian flag here

Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a minaret and "victory tower" that forms part of the Qutb complex,a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Mehrauli area of New Delhi,India

Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri is a town in the Agra District of Uttar Pradesh,India.The city itself was founded as the capital of Mughal Empire in 1571 by Emperor Akbar

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal is also known as the "Palace of Winds,"is a palace in Jaipur in India,known for its unique five-story exterior with 953 small windows

Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building in Kolkata, West Bengal India,which was built between 1906 and 1921 in memory of Queen Victoria

Humayun's Tomb

Humayun's tomb is the tomb of the Mughal Emperor Humayun in Delhi,India.The tomb was commissioned by Humayun's first wife and chief consort,Empress Bega Begum,in 1569-70

Khajuraho Temples

The Khajuraho Group of Monuments is a group of Hindu and Jain temples in Madhya Pradesh State in India.They are famous for their nagara-style architectural symbolism and their erotic sculptures

Amer Fort

Amer Fort is a fort located in Amer,Rajasthan State in India.Amer is a town with an area of 4 square kilometres located 11 kilometres from Jaipur,the capital of Rajasthan

India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial located in New Delhi,built in honor of the soldiers who died during World War I and the Third Anglo-Afghan War

Meenakshi Amman Temple

Meenakshi Amman Temple is a historic Hindu temple located on the southern bank of the Vaigai River in the temple city of Madurai,Tamil Nadu State in India

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple is also known as Harmandir Sahib,is a gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar,Punjab in India.It is the preeminent spiritual site of Sikhism

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves are a network of sculpted caves located on Elephanta Island in Mumbai Harbour,10 kilometres to the east of the city of Mumbai in the Indian state of Maharashtra

Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort is one of the largest forts in India.Built around 1459 by Rao Jodha,the fort is situated 410 feet above the city and is enclosed by imposing thick walls

Charminar

The Charminar is a monument and mosque located in Hyderabad,Telangana State in India.The landmark has become a global icon of Hyderabad,listed among the most recognized structures of India

Jallianwala Bagh

Jallianwala Bagh is a public garden in Amritsar in the Punjab state of India and houses a memorial of national importance,established in 1951 to commemorate the massacre of peaceful protesters by British forces on April 13,1919

Sanchi Stupa

The Great Stupa at Sanchi is one of the oldest stone structures in India and an important monument of Indian Architecture.It was commissioned by the emperor Ashoka in the 3rd century BCE

Ajanta and Ellora Caves

The Ajanta and Ellora caves are rock-cut caves located near Aurangabad in Maharashtra,India.They are considered to be one of the finest examples of ancient rock-cut caves & are renowned for their beautiful sculptures & paintings

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace is also known as Amba Vilas Palace.It is a historical palace and a royal residence at Mysore in the southern Karnataka state,India

Hampi

Hampi is located in Karnataka,India.It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.It was one of the richest & largest cities in the world during its prime as the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th century
